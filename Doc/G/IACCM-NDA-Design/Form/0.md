Contract Name: Non-Disclosure Agreement

Doc.Ti=Non-Disclosure Agreement

This.sec=This Non-Disclosure Agreement is made on {EffectiveDate.YMD} (the "{_Effective_Date}") between

Def.Ti=Defined terms

Def.Confidential_Information.sec={_Confidential_Information}: Non-public information belonging to a party. Trade secrets, information concerning business models, strategies, designs, customers and suppliers, consumer personal data and pricing in all cases are examples of {_Confidential_Information}.

Note=Specify the specific activities to be undertaken by one or both of the parties for which {_Confidential_Information} is shared.

Def.Purpose.sec={_Purpose}: {Purpose.sec}

Def.Discloser.sec={_Discloser}: The party providing {_Confidential_Information} to a {_Recipient} during discussions or activities associated with the {_Purpose}.

Def.Recipient.sec={_Recipient}: The party receiving {_Confidential_Information} from a {_Discloser}.

Def.sec=<ol><li>{Def.Confidential_Information.sec}<li>{Def.Purpose.sec}<li>{Def.Discloser.sec}<li>{Def.Recipient.sec}</ol>

Def.=[G/Z/ol/s4]

1.Sec={Def.Sec}

2.Ti={_Confidential_Information}
	
2.1.Ti=Exchange of information

2.1.1.sec=The parties agree to exchange {_Confidential_Information} for the {_Purpose}.

2.1.2.sec=The {_Recipient} will protect the {_Confidential_Information} in accordance with this agreement.

2.1.3.sec=Each party retains all interest and proprietary rights in the {_Confidential_Information} it discloses and in any information or materials that it or the other party derives from that {_Confidential_Information}.

2.1.=[G/Z/ol/s3]

2.2.Ti=Excluded information

2.2.0.sec=This agreement’s restrictions on the use and disclosure of {_Confidential_Information} do not apply to information that, without the breach of this agreement,

2.2.1.sec=is already known to the {_Recipient},

2.2.2.sec=is or becomes publicly known,

2.2.3.sec=is or subsequently comes into the possession of the {_Recipient} from a third party without restrictions,

2.2.4.sec=is independently developed by the {_Recipient} without the use of {_Confidential_Information}, or

2.2.5.sec=is expressly indicated as not confidential by the {_Discloser}.

2.2.=[G/Z/ol/s5]

2.3.Ti=Burden of proof

2.3.sec=The {_Recipient} has the burden of proof relating to all exceptions to the definition of {_Confidential_Information}.
	
2.4.Ti=Use of information

2.4.1.sec=The {_Recipient} will hold in confidence all {_Confidential_Information} and use it solely for the {_Purpose}.

2.4.2.sec=If the {_Discloser}’s {_Confidential_Information} is incorporated into documents created by the {_Recipient}, the portions of the new document containing the {_Confidential_Information} are protected in accordance with this Non-Disclosure Agreement.

2.4.=[G/Z/ol/s2]

2.=[G/Z/ol/4]

2.5.Ti=Standard of care

2.5.sec=The {_Recipient} including any of its affiliates will protect the {_Confidential_Information} with reasonable care and as if it were its own
	
2.6.Ti=Duration of confidentiality

2.6.0.sec=The confidentiality obligation will continue indefinitely for:

2.6.1.sec=Trade secrets

2.6.2.sec=Consumer personal information

2.6.3.sec=Proprietary software code

2.6.00.sec=The confidentiality obligation will last three years, starting when {_Confidential_Information} is disclosed, for all other {_Confidential_Information}.

2.6.=[G/Z/ol/s3]

2.7.Ti=Return or destruction of {_Confidential_Information}

2.7.1.0.sec=At the {_Discloser}'s request, the {_Recipient} will promptly

2.7.1.1.sec=return to the {_Discloser} all {_Confidential_Information} provided by the {_Discloser}, or

2.7.1.2.sec=destroy all copies it made of any {_Confidential_Information}.

2.7.2.sec=If requested by the {_Discloser}, the {_Recipient} will deliver to the {_Discloser} a certificate executed by the {_Recipient} confirming compliance with the return or destruction obligation under this section.

2.7.=[G/Z/ol/s2]

2.=[G/Z/ol/7]

3.Ti=Disclosure of Confidential Information
	
3.1.Ti=Permitted disclosure

3.1.0.sec=The {_Recipient} may disclose {_Confidential_Information} to its officers, directors, employees, affiliates, representatives, agents and subcontractors who

3.1.1.sec=have a need-to-know in furtherance of the {_Purpose},

3.1.2.sec=have been informed of the confidentiality obligations of this  agreement, and

3.1.3.sec=agree to be bound by confidentiality obligations at least as stringent as those contained in this agreement.

3.1.00.sec=The {_Recipient} remains responsible for any acts or omissions of those persons or entities.

3.1.=[G/Z/ol/s3]

3.2.Ti=Required disclosure

3.2.1.Ti=Limited disclosure

3.2.1.sec=The {_Recipient} may disclose {_Confidential_Information} if it is compelled to do so by law or regulation. The {_Recipient} may in such case disclose the requested {_Confidential_Information}, but only to the extent necessary to satisfy the request.

3.2.2.Ti=Notification of required disclosure

3.2.2.sec=Before disclosure, the {_Recipient} will give the {_Discloser} prompt written notice (if it is legally permitted to do so) and reasonably cooperate with the {_Discloser} in seeking a protective order or other appropriate remedy.

3.2.=[G/Z/ol/2]

3.=[G/Z/ol/2]

4.Ti=Unauthorized disclosure and confidentiality breach
	
4.1.Ti=Notification of unauthorized disclosure

4.1.sec=The {_Recipient} will promptly notify the {_Discloser} if it discovers any loss or unauthorized disclosure of {_Confidential_Information}.

4.2.Ti=Equitable remedies

4.2.1.Ti=Acknowledgment of irreparable harm

4.2.1.sec=The parties acknowledge that breach or threatened breach of any of the obligations in this agreement would result in irreparable harm to the non-breaching party that cannot be adequately relieved solely by monetary damages.

4.2.2.Ti=Equitable remedies

4.2.2.sec=The parties agree that a party facing a threatened or actual breach of this agreement by the other party may request from a court any applicable equitable remedies, including injunctive relief.

4.2.=[G/Z/ol/2]

4.=[G/Z/ol/2]

5.Ti=General provisions

5.1.Ti=Notices

5.1.1.Ti=Method of notice

5.1.1.0.sec=The parties will give all notices and communications between the parties in writing by

5.1.1.1.sec=personal delivery,

5.1.1.2.sec=a nationally-recognized, next-day courier service, or

5.1.1.3.sec=first-class registered or certified mail postage prepaid

5.1.1.00.sec=to the party's address specified in this agreement, or to the address that a party has notified to be its address for the purposes of this section.

5.1.1.=[G/Z/ol/s3]

5.1.2.Ti=Receipt of notice

5.1.2.0.sec=A notice given under this agreement will be effective

5.1.2.1.sec=when the other party receives it, or

5.1.2.2.sec=if mailed, on the earlier of the day the other party receives it and the fifth business day after its mailing.

5.1.2.=[G/Z/ol/s2]

5.1.=[G/Z/ol/2]

5.2.Ti=Entire agreement

5.2.sec=This agreement represents the entire understanding between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes any previous communication or agreements that may exist.

5.3.Ti=Counterparts

5.3.sec=This Agreement may be signed in counterparts, each of which will be deemed to be an original, but all of which, taken together, constitute one and the same agreement.

5.4.Ti=Assignment

5.4.sec=Neither party may assign this agreement without the other party's written consent.

5.5.Ti=Amendment

5.5.sec=This agreement can be amended only by a document in writing signed by both parties.

5.6.Ti=Waiver

5.6.sec=One party’s failure or neglect to enforce any of its rights under this agreement will not be deemed to be a waiver of that party's rights.

5.7.Ti=Severability

5.7.sec=If any part of this agreement is declared unenforceable or invalid, the remainder will continue to be valid and enforceable.

5.8.Ti=Governing law

5.8.sec=This agreement will be governed, construed, and enforced in accordance with the laws of {Law.Country}, without regard to its conflict of laws rules.

5.9.Ti=Termination

5.9.sec=Either party may terminate this agreement upon written notice to the other party.

5.10.Ti=Ownership

5.10.sec=Each party retains all interest and proprietary rights in the {_Confidential_Information} it discloses and in any information or materials that it or the other party derives from that {_Confidential_Information}.

5.11.Ti=No Warranty

5.11.sec=Neither party gives any warranties with respect to the {_Confidential_Information} it shares.

5.=[G/Z/ol/11]

This agreement has been signed by the parties.
