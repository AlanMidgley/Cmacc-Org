This set of computable contract materials restates many of the resources at https://www.ga4gh.org/genomic-data-toolkit/regulatory-ethics-toolkit/
The "Demo" demonstrates a system of patient consents as objects that inherit from the GA4GH model patient consent, but with informal translations into French and German, and with a study expressed as an object. This keeps the consents substantially standardized and computable even as it enables needed variations.
